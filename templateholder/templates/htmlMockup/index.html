Common Questions

What is this for?

This is for users to easily search for their preferred breed of cats and/or dogs they are looking to adopt

How to filter the search?

The questionnaire page serves as a way for users to filter the cat/dog search results, they answer based on personality, living conditions, and their wants.

Where to view all pets?

Users can also browse our database, which contains descpriptions of specific cat/dog breeds and where they can adopt them.

Why Sign up?

Signing up grants the user the ability to add animal breeds to the database, this is mainly for adoption agencies to use.
